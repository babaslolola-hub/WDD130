Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is Nigeria's largest city, a major financial hub, and one of the fastest-growing urban areas in Africa. Originally a Yoruba fishing settlement known as Eko, it later served as the nation's capital until 1991. Today, it spans islands and mainland districts, acting as West Africa's economic, cultural, and technological engine
